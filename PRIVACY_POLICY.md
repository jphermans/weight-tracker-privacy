# Weight Tracker Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Weight Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

This policy applies to the Weight Tracker mobile application available on iOS devices.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Weight entries**: Date, time, weight measurements, and optional notes you enter
- **App settings**: Unit preferences (kg/lbs), language, color scheme
- **Goal settings**: Target weight, start weight, and optional target date

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifier
- **App usage data**: App usage patterns, error logs, and performance data
- **iCloud backup data**: Only if you enable iCloud backup (see Section 4)

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Health data beyond weight measurements you voluntarily enter
- Payment information
- Third-party app data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purpose
- To provide the core weight tracking functionality you requested
- To store and display your weight history and progress charts
- To calculate and show insights about your weight trends

### 3.2 App Features
- Display weight entries in charts and lists
- Calculate progress toward weight goals
- Generate insights and motivation messages
- Export data in JSON format (when requested)

### 3.3 Technical Purposes
- Ensure app stability and performance
- Debug and fix technical issues
- Improve user experience

### 3.4 Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable data protection laws
- Respond to legal requests (only when legally required)

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
All your weight data is stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up.

### 4.2 iCloud Backup (Optional)
If you enable iCloud backup in the app settings:
- Your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
- Data is accessible only by you and only on your devices
- We do not have access to your iCloud backup data
- You can disable iCloud backup at any time

### 4.3 Data Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All local data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- **Access Controls**: Optional PIN/Face ID protection for app access
- **No Cloud Processing**: Data is never processed on external servers
- **No Third-Party Access**: Your data is never shared with third parties

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described below.

### 5.2 Limited Sharing Scenarios
- **Legal Requirements**: Only if required by law and with proper legal process
- **App Store Compliance**: Anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to comply with App Store requirements
- **Error Reporting**: Anonymous error reports to improve app stability (no personal data included)

### 5.3 Third-Party Services
The app integrates with:
- **Apple iCloud**: For optional backup (your data stays in your iCloud account)
- **No other third-party services**: No analytics, advertising, or tracking services

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Data Access and Control
- **View Data**: Access all your weight entries within the app
- **Export Data**: Export your data as JSON file at any time
- **Delete Data**: Delete individual entries or clear all data
- **Disable Backup**: Turn off iCloud backup at any time

### 6.2 Data Deletion
- Delete the app to permanently remove all local data
- Disable iCloud backup and delete backup data from your iCloud account
- Contact us to request data deletion (though we don't store your data)

### 6.3 Opt-Out Rights
- Disable iCloud backup in app settings
- Clear all app data in device settings
- Delete the app from your device

## 7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Since all your data is stored locally on your device and optionally in your iCloud account, no international data transfers occur through our service.

## 9. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you delete the app or clear app data
- **iCloud Backup**: Retained according to your iCloud storage plan and settings
- **Error Logs**: Automatically cleaned up after 30 days
- **No Server Storage**: We do not retain your data on our servers

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
